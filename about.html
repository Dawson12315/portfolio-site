My Story

I transitioned into software engineering after years in leadership and operations roles, including mission-critical environments in the U.S. Air Force, and maintaining high-volume work environments in my position at the Discount Tire Company. That background shaped how I work: I value reliability, clear communication, and owning problems end-to-end.

I earned a Software Engineering Certificate from Noble Desktop, with training in HTML/CSS, Node/Express, MongoDB/Mongoose, React, Javascript, Python/Django, and API development.

Today, I build backend services and full-stack projects using Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and Python/Django — with an emphasis on clean architecture, readable code, and practical solutions.
